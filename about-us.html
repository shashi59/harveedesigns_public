About Us

Harvee's Nest is an Early Childhood Nurture and Activity Centre that operates on providing a safe and secure progressive environment for 10 months to 5 years old children. With nearly 20,000 sq. feet of space, Harvee's Nest is one of the quick rising Nurturing Centre. It has a motivating atmosphere which stimulates the children to explore, share and learn. There are various activities which have been carefully crafted to help them learn to be independent, cooperative, competitive and smart.

Without exception, parents desire to bestow the best of all things for their children. Despite the fact that parental care could not be replaced, we at Harvee's Nest assure to present a protective nest for your children.
